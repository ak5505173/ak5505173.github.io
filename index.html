Power BIDAXData ModelingDashboard
// Project Overview

Interactive Power BI sales dashboard for Motorola's mobile product line — analyzing transactional data across Indian cities to uncover regional demand patterns, payment trends, customer ratings, and brand-level revenue performance.

// Technical Stack

• Power BI for end-to-end dashboard design, data modeling, and interactive visualizations

• Slicers & filters for Mobile Model, Payment Method, Brand, and Day Name drill-downs

// Impact

Delivered a multi-view dashboard covering sales by city, monthly quantity trends, customer ratings distribution, payment method breakdown (UPI, Debit, Cash, Credit Card), and brand comparison across Xiaomi, Vivo, OnePlus, Samsung, and Apple — enabling data-driven decisions for regional sales strategy.
